Must-see in Aytos

'The eagle' monument

The monument is located on a hill overlooking the city and offers a panoramic view of Aytos and the surrounding area. The city gets its name from the old Greek word for eagle, "Aetos".

Ethnographic complex "Gengera"

This complex is a museum that showcases the traditional lifestyle, culture, and crafts of the region. It is located in the entrance of the city's park "Slaveeva reka".

Celebration of the town's day

Aytossians celebrate their town's day on the 26th of October with traditional outdoor festivities, including music, dance, and food. The event is a great opportunity to experience the local culture and meet the residents.
